Our Plumbing Services

From emergency drain cleaning to sewer line repair — Doctor Rooter 911 has the experience and equipment to solve any plumbing problem fast.

Plumbing Repair

Leaks, burst pipes, fixtures, water heaters — our licensed plumbers diagnose and fix problems fast with upfront pricing and no surprise fees.

Drain Cleaning

Slow or clogged drains? We use professional hydro-jetting and snaking equipment to clear kitchen sinks, bathrooms, floor drains, and more.

Sewer Cleaning

Main sewer line backups are serious. Our powerful sewer jetting equipment removes roots, grease, and debris from your main line — fast.

TV Camera Inspection

We use advanced video camera technology to inspect sewer and drain lines — finding cracks, roots, and blockages without guesswork or digging.

Water Mitigation

Flooding or water damage? We respond fast to extract water, dry affected areas, and prevent mold — protecting your home or business.

Porta Potty Rental

Need temporary sanitation for events, construction, or projects? We offer reliable porta potty rental throughout southern Wisconsin.
