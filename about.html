About Doctor Rooter 911

Madison's most trusted plumbing company — built on honest work, fast service, and a deep commitment to the communities we serve.

Built on Three Decades of Trust

Doctor Rooter 911 was founded by John Werkmeister over 30 years ago to give Wisconsin a plumber they could rely on. Today we serve Madison, Milwaukee, Janesville, and Beloit with licensed pros who show up when we say and charge what we quote.

We're still the first call for families who've trusted us for decades. That loyalty comes from doing good work, being honest, and always showing up.

Doctor Rooter 911 was founded by John Werkmeister with a simple mission: give Wisconsin homeowners and businesses a plumber they could actually rely on. One who shows up when they say, charges what they quoted, and doesn't cut corners.

What started as a local Madison drain cleaning service has grown over 30+ years into one of southern Wisconsin's most recognized plumbing companies — serving Madison, Milwaukee, Janesville, and Beloit with a team of experienced, licensed professionals.

We've unclogged thousands of drains, repaired hundreds of sewer lines, and responded to countless emergencies in the middle of the night — because that's what neighbors do for each other. This isn't just a business to us. It's a relationship with our community that we take seriously every single day.

We're proud to still be the first call for families who called us 20 years ago. That kind of loyalty doesn't happen by accident — it happens by doing good work, being honest, and always showing up.
